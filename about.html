Hi, my name is Nikita Petrov. I’m a Russian artist and writer living in Málaga, Spain.

Every day I go through the news and collect what’s compelling in Consensus Reality. As the headlines lose their relevance, I erase them. Some survive for a long time because they express something meaningful.

Every two weeks I talk to my friend, Boris Shoshitaishvili (Berggruen, Our Planetary Mirror) on a public livestream. Our chats are unstructured and range widely; sometimes we’re joined by callers. We try to articulate ideas on our minds, see how they interact and change one another, and what new ones emerge. We call the show Kosmopolitika.

On alternating weeks, I talk to author & journalist Robert Wright on his podcast, Nonzero.

The most interesting ideas that emerge from these conversations get catalogued, described, illustrated, and become the cards that you see above.

(I also produce Glenn Loury’s weekly livestream.)

Paralysis & Hallucinations are excerpts from my dream journal.
